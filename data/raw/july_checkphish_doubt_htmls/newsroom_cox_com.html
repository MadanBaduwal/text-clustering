Welcome to the Cox Communications Newsroom, the best source for company information and related content. For those unfamiliar with Cox, it is the largest private telecom company in the U.S., with nearly $12 billion in annual revenue. Cox connects 6 million residential and business customers to the things they care about most via advanced digital video, internet, phone and home security and automation services.
